Project Details

The Climate Change Advisor, also known as the Post-Climate Change Survival Advisor, envisions a world deeply affected by climate change. Acting as a digital survival advisor from the future, it provides users with scenario-based guidance, survival strategies, and location-specific advice. This project raises awareness about the consequences of climate change while showcasing the potential of advanced AI to address global challenges.

The chatbot allows users to explore scenarios such as rising sea levels, prolonged droughts, and extreme weather events. Through interactive conversations, it offers educational insights, survival tips, and scenario-specific advice, encouraging proactive thought and environmental action.

Speculative design plays a key role in this project, creating a narrative that combines real-world data and futuristic storytelling. By immersing users in a climate-changed future, the chatbot inspires reflection on current practices and collective solutions to mitigate future environmental disasters.
